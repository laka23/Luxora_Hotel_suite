About Luxora Hotel suite

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 1985, Luxora Hotel suite has been setting the standard for luxury hospitality for over three decades. Our commitment to exceptional service and attention to detail has made us a favorite among discerning travelers.

Each of our 120 rooms and suites has been meticulously designed to blend contemporary elegance with timeless comfort, offering stunning views of the city skyline or the tranquil harbor.
